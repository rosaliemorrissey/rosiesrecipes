Hello! I am Rosalie. I have been an avid baker for about as long as I can remember. While over the last decade my family will tell you my skills and results have improved immensely, the joy I have gotten from trying something new and creating something that brought joy to others has remained constant. On the site you will find a variety of recipes. Some of these recipes pre-date me and are long-held family recipes, while others came from my own experimentation.

Baker's Note: Baking is very much a science, and the day I started approaching a recipe like a lab instructions was a highly successful day in my baking journey. That being said the environment you bake in can deeply impact your success. Think about if you are at a high elevation, using a different material for your pans, and please use an oven thermometer.

Allergen Note: One of my main test tasters has always been my mom who is strictly gluten free. These recipes can be adapted to accomadate gluten allergies, make sure to use a gluten free flour marked "measure for measure" if you would like to go the more risky route and use one not marked for measure for measure use make sure to add the right amount of xanthum gum. Many of these recipes use nuts, or products typically manufactured in nut facilities, you can skip the nuts all together or use another mix-in such as dried fruit.

Happy Baking!
