Zucchini Bread

Ingredients

Batter

1.5 cups all-purpose flour (about 5 medium bananas)

0.5 teaspoon baking powder

0.5 teaspoon baking soda

0.5 teaspoon salt

2 teaspoon cinnamon

0.5 teaspoon nutmeg

1 cup chocolate chips

1 cup chopped walnuts

0.5 cup butter (melted and cooled)

0.5 cup brown sugar

0.5 cup sugar

1 egg

1teaspoon vanilla extract

2zucchini (grated, looking for 1 heaping cup)

Directions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and prepare a loaf pan with cooking spray.

Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg to a large bowl. Add chocolate chips and walnuts, if using. Whisk to combine and set aside.

In another bowl, add the melted (and cooled) butter, brown sugar, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, and grated zucchini. Mix until everything is combined.

Fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just combined. Pour the batter in the prepared loaf pan. Bake for 50-55 minutes, until the top of the bread is golden brown and firm to the touch.

Let the bread cool before slicing.

Happy Baking!
